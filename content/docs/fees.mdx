# Fees

Sew Protocol fees are bounded and transparent.

## Fee Principles

- Fees are capped at protocol level
- Fees are known at escrow creation
- Escrow fees are deducted upfront
- Yield and appeal fees are taken from generated amounts

## Fee Types

### Escrow Creation Fee

A small fee is charged when creating an escrow to prevent spam and fund protocol development.

### Yield Protocol Fee

When yield is generated on escrowed funds, a portion goes to the protocol.

- Fee is snapshotted at escrow creation
- Cannot change during escrow's lifetime

### Appeal Bond Fee

When a dispute resolution is appealed, the appellant posts a bond.

- A portion may be taken as protocol fee
- Discourages frivolous appeals
- Currently inactive at launch

## Fee Summary

| Fee Type        | Default | Max | Applied When       |
| --------------- | ------- | --- | ------------------ |
| Escrow creation | 1%      | 2%  | At escrow creation |
| Yield protocol  | 30%     | 30% | On generated yield |
| Appeal bond     | 0%      | 10% | On appeal bonds    |

## Fee Recipient

All fees go to a designated fee address controlled by governance.

This funds:

- Protocol development
- Security audits
- Ecosystem growth
